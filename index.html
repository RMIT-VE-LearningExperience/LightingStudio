Lighting Studio

An interactive 3D environment for learning professional lighting — for screen, stage and photography.

Light Direction

Discover how angle, distance and placement sculpt shape, shadow and depth on a subject.

Colour Temperature

Explore the Kelvin scale — from warm tungsten glow to cool daylight — and how it sets mood and atmosphere.

Three-Point Setup

Build a classic key, fill and rim configuration — the foundation of professional portrait and product lighting.

Equipment Operation

Operate fresnel spots, softboxes, octaboxes, parcans and LED panels — understanding each unit's output and character.

Light Quality

Compare hard and soft sources — inverse square falloff, shadow edge sharpness, and softness from modifier size.

Contrast Ratio

Control the key-to-fill ratio to dial in mood — from flat, even illumination to dramatic high-contrast looks.
